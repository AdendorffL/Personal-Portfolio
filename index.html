About Me

I am Lourens Adendorff, a Full-Stack Developer, working primarily with Node.js and .NET. I focus on building systems that are structured, maintainable, and designed to scale - because software should age well, not mysteriously.

I care about clean architecture, predictable behavior, and writing code that makes sense long after the sprint ends. Most of my goes into ensuring that business logic is clear, testable, and resilient under real-world conditions.

I approach development pragmatically: clarity of cleverness, structure over shortcuts, and solutions that hold up outside of development environments. I'm continuously refining my understanding of system design, performance optimization, and production-ready engineering practices.

I build the parts people don't see - but rely on every time they click something.
